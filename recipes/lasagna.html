Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a hearty and flavorful baked pasta dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy cheeses, and savory seasonings.

The dish typically includes layers of pasta, a tomato-based sauce with ground beef and/or sausage, and a mixture of cheeses such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. As it bakes, the cheese melts and blends with the sauce, creating a warm, bubbling dish with a golden, slightly crispy top. Each bite combines tender pasta, rich sauce, and creamy cheese, making lasagna a filling and comforting meal often enjoyed at family dinners or special gatherings.

  • Prep Time: 45 mins
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr 30 mins
  • Servings: 8

Note: Double the recipe to increase the serving size

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs lean ground meat (ie: Hamburger or pork)
  • 136 oz of lasgna sauce
  • 8 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 8 cups mozzarello cheese
  • 8 cups colbyjack cheese
  • 32 oz of lasagna noodles

Instructions

  1. For the Meat Sauce, brown the meat in a large pot and once the meat is browned drain the oil.
  2. Add the Lasgna Sauce to the drained meat and heat on high until the sauce starts a slow boil. lower the heat to medium low and allow to simmer for 30 minutes.

Build Lasagna (3 - 9 X 13 pans):

  1. Spread 1 1/2 cups of the sauce in bottom of each of the 13x9-inch baking dish's.
  2. Spread a layer of the lasagna noodles across the sauce.
  3. Distribute 1/3 of the cheddar cheese in each of the pans to cover the lasagna noodles.
  4. Repeat building two more layers, using the colbyjack cheese and ending with the mozzarello cheese on top.
  5. Spray one side of a large sheet of Al-foil with no stick cooking spray. Cover lasagna with foil, sprayed-side down.

Bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil. Then bake 15 minutes longer, or until cheese is lightly browned. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
